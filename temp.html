Indrayani Vidya Mandir (IVM):

  • IVM was established in 1965 & stands as one of the pioneer institutes of this region imparting quality education.
  • IVM came into existence by the initiative taken by eminent personalities like Acharya P. K. Atre, Dr. M. M. Altekar & others.
  • IVM has been blessed with towering personalities like Prof. Ramkrishna More, Balasaheb Kibe, Dadasaheb Dhore, Balashaheb Barmukh.
  • The IVM Talegaon Dabhade campus is spread over 15 acres.
